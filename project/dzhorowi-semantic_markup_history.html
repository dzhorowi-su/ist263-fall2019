History of the Automobile

History

1885 Karl Benz develops the first-ever automobile. His three-wheeled Motorwagen was powered by the first internal combustion engine
1908 The Ford Model T was the first mass-produced automobile. It was marketed to the middle class and featured interchangeable parts.
1913 Henry Ford creates the first assembly line for the Model T. The production time to build a Model T went from 12 hours to 2 hours and 30 minutes per car.
1997 The first Hybrid automobile created was the Toyota Prius. It featured both a gas combustion engine as well as electric motors and instantly became a worldwide success.
